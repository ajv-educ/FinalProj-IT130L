League of Legends


With more than 140 champions, you’ll find the perfect match for your playstyle.

Master one, or master them all!

BE GOD-LIKE

Be tactical in this team-based strategy game where two teams of five powerful champions face off to destroy the other’s base. Choose from over 140 champions to make epic plays, secure kills, and take down towers as you battle your way to victory.


CHOOSE YOUR LANE

There are five positions that make up the recommended team comp for the game. Each lane lends itself to certain kinds of champions and roles—try them all or lock in to the lane that calls you.

CLEAR THE PATH

Your team needs to clear at least one lane to get to the enemy Nexus. Blocking your path are defense structures called turrets and inhibitors. Each lane has three turrets and one inhibitor, and each Nexus is guarded by two turrets.

DESTROY THE BASE

The Nexus is the heart of both teams’ bases. Destroy the enemy’s Nexus first to win the game.



CHOOSE YOUR ROLE

Who will you take into the fight?


SUPPORT

The Carry

Help facilitate vision control, protect your ADC, and provide game-changing crowd control.

MARKSMAN

The ADC

Sacrifice defensive power and utility to focus on dealing strong, continuous damage to individual targets.

MAGE

The Magician

Use your great reach, ability-based area of effect damage and crowd control in tandem with each other to trap and destroy enemies from a distance.

TANK

The Frontline Champ

Excel at disrupting enemies and diverting focus to themselves. Lock down specific targets (or several targets at once), as well as remove (or peel) threats from allies.

ASSASSIN

The Agile Champion

Eliminate key targets or sources of high damage output to easily overpower the enemy team.




What is League of Legends?

League of Legends is a team-based strategy game where two teams of five powerful champions face off to destroy the other's base. Choose from over 140 champions to make epic plays, secure kills, and take down towers as you battle your way to victory.

What platforms is LoL available for? Is it cross-platform?

The game is available on Android and IOS pc.

What languages does LoL support?

LoL currently supports English, German, Spanish, French, Italian, Czech, Greek, Hungarian, Polish, Romanian, Portuguese, Turkish, Russioan, Spanish, and Japanese. We’re working on additional languages and beyond—more info to come!

What is Wild Rift?

Dive into Wild Rift: the skills-and-strats 5v5 MOBA experience of League of Legends, now built from the ground up for mobile and console. With fresh controls and fast-paced games, players of every level can team up with friends, lock in their champion, and go for the big plays.
